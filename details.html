Product Details

The TAG Heuer Men's CAU1115.BA0869 Formula 1 Stainless Steel Ceramic Chronograph Watch is a sleek and sophisticated timepiece made for a man with discriminating taste. Featuring a grey analog chronograph dial, date display, black bezel with tachymeter scale and a silver stainless steel band with black ceramic center links, this watch is an elegant addition to your wardrobe and watch collection.

Stainless steel case with a stainless steel bracelet with black ceramic center links. Fixed black ceramic bezel with tachymeter. Anthracite sunray dial with silver-tone hands and index hour markers. Minute markers around the outer rim. Dial Type: Analog. Luminescent hands and markers. Date display at the 4 o'clock position. Chronograph - three sub-dials displaying: 60 second, 30 minute and 1/10th of a second. Quartz movement. Scratch resistant sapphire crystal. Screw down crown. Solid case back. Case diameter: 42 mm. Case thickness: 12 mm. Round case shape. Band width: 20 mm. Deployment clasp with push button release. Water resistant at 200 meters / 660 feet. Functions: chronograph, date, hour, minute, second. Casual watch style. Watch label: Swiss Made. Tag Heuer Formula 1 Anthracite Sunray Dial Mens Watch CAU1115.BA0869.
